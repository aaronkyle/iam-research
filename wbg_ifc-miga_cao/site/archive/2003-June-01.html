Welcome to the Office of the Compliance Advisor/Ombudsman (CAO) for the International Finance Corporation (IFC) and Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency (MIGA).

We hope that this web site will provide you with the information you need to lodge a complaint if you are impacted directly by an IFC or MIGA project.

We also hope that it will provide information on the objectives and roles of the CAO for those in the private sector, civil society, and public institutions interested in the challenges of accountability, transparency and responsiveness as we strive for more equitable and sustainable development.

The Final Report of the Review of IFC's Safeguard Policies has been cleared by the President and is forthcoming. We will post it to this website shortly. Please click on Safeguard Policy Review at right, under Features, to read the Review. A Spanish translation of the Report is forthcoming, and will be posted here on the website as soon as it is completed.
