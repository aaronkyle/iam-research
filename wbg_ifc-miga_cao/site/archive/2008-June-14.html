Welcome to the Office of the Compliance Advisor Ombudsman (CAO), the independent recourse mechanism for the private sector arms of the World Bank Group:

 

  • International Finance Corporation (IFC); and

 

  • Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency (MIGA)

 

This website provides information on how to file a complaint to the CAO if you believe you are, or may be, negatively affected by an IFC or MIGA project.

Active ombudsman complaints and compliance audits can be tracked on their individual webpages. All CAO public documents for closed complaints, audits, and advisory projects are located in the archive.

NOTE: If you are affected by an International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) or International Development Association (IDA) project, please contact the Inspection Panel.
