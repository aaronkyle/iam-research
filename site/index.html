Independent Accountability Mechanisms (IAMs)

 

The term 'independent accountability mechanisms' (IAMs) refers to governance instruments established among international financial institutions (IFIs) to provide recourse for social and environmental harms resulting from IFI-funded projects.

This site contains information about IAMs and focuses particularly on IAMnet members, as identified in the table below:
